ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Tajah! I have been working as a middle school social studies (world cultures to be specific) teacher in Houston, Texas for the past five years. Prior to teaching, I attended and graduated from Texas A&M Universtiy at College Station with a degree in Economics. I'm currently attending a coding boot camp with the University of Texas- Houston to help guide me to a career in web development and this site was created by me for practice.

When I am not teaching or practicing coding, I am working out, practicing yoga, or traveling. Traveling is probably my favorite hobby and I hope to see as much of the world as I can.
