ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Tajah! I have been working as a middle school social studies (world cultures) teacher in Houston, Texas for the past five years. Prior to teaching, I attended and graduated from Texas A&M Universtiy at College Station with a degree in Economics. I'm currently attending a coding boot camp with the University of Texas- Houston to help guide me towards a career in web development and this site was created by me.

When I am not teaching or coding, I am working out, practicing yoga, or traveling. Traveling is probably my favorite hobby and I hope to see as much of the world as I can. My goal is to travel to every continent, except Antarctica, by the age of 30.

Thank you for taking the time to view my site. Enjoy!
